Let me introduce the web site

First of all thanks to visit my web page. Just a quick introduction to the web site, what there is inside and why I did it.

At the beginning I thought that for a designer having a web page in order to show his works, CV and portfolio was mandatory. Thus I decided to begin my own one (and of course in the hardest way, Html, Css and Js, no shortcut), but after a while I ran out of time so I put it on hold. But during those last weeks, with a little bit of time, I decided to continue my work and now, finally, the website is on!!

Here you can find literaly myself, who I really am, what I love, what I'm doing, where I'm now and of course I encourage you to contact me.

Thus, I think that the introduction ends here, I wish you to enjoy your self discovering the site, and don't forget to follow me on socials! 😉
