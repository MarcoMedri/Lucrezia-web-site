Short presentation

My name Is Lucrezia, I was born in Italy in 1991, more precizely in Colle Val d'Elsa, a small city in Tuscany, really close to the more famouse Siena. There I took the first steps. Like most of the italian I did my kindergarten, my elementary, middle and also the superior school there. That allowed me to build really strong friendships that nowadays still hold on (despite me ☺). After that I felt my town too small for me, I was eager to live new experience, discover other places, other reality, so I travelled to Milan in order to start my university career as Designer!

There I lived 5 years unforgettable, I've had a lot of amazing experience. The university was tough and drained a lot of energy, but my study mates always cheered me up (or at least the tried).

Graduation done, so next step? Of course, I needed to find a job, so after some research, I found my next spot. Cesena!

There I could conciliate two of my best passions design and fitness. I started working for Technogym, at the beginning has been hard, new environment, new job, I knew noone (yet), but I was too much excited. Live there changed my life, I met really valid co-workers, I learned a lot from them someone is still an inspiration for me. But most of all I found amazing people, some of them are still my friends and really good ones, those kind that when you come back, time seems to never went on. I had friends, I had the sea (you still miss me ☹), but I wanted also to try, try new adventures, live different cultures, test myself in tough situations, so I decided to move for the umpteenth time, so France I'm coming!

As designer

I have always loved when useful things are also beautiful and simple and that the reason that pushed me to study design, in order to be able to create new beautiful things. For me design is more related to a point of view, I like beautiful stuff in general, natural landscape, small handmade objects, all days environments. Everything is a piece of design if viewed in a wider way. I like when stuff are made in the best way, cause when it happens, you can feel it, you perceive that something is perfect, it is like it was supposed to be. I hope to carry out something good in that way in the future, currently I'm still learning, observing and trying.

Like every good designer I really love photography. I'm very luky cause my uncle gifted me a camera and ever since I'm trying to imrove my skills, unfortunatly time is never too much so my level is always below my expectation, but as always I'm positive, I will do also this!

P.S. If some one is interested Netflix as a lot of good documentary relative to design, I love them.

As "fit/sport" girl

I love practice every kind of sport, I never step back in front of new opportunities. I usually prefer team workouts instead of individual ones, I think that share the suffering allow you to go further your limits and of course it's good meet new people as well.
I also love practise out of the gym breathe fresh and clean air is amazing. So I go running expecially, even though right now I'm not in my best shape, but in summer I love more beach sports, and of course beachvolley is my favourite one (to be honest I have played volleyball for several years, maybe that is the reason).
Beyond the pure power and tonicity of the muscles, I belive that flexibility is essential for an healthy life, so I started to be passionate about yoga and pilates, moreover after a while I decided to try to become an instructor. Therefore I started a very good crush course in Bologna and in the end I obtained my own certificate, and now I have the licence to teach it.
I would like also to do something specific for the yoga part, cause the spiritual part related to this discipline is amazing, even though now I don't have the right amount of time to invest in this activicty that is all but easy to learn properly. I approached this discipline cause it makes me feel better both from physical and spiritual point of view.

Last but not least I try to train a lot cause I really love food, so I hope that this activity gives me the possibility to eat more and don't became fatter then how I am right now.
Jokes a part, physical activity allow me to feel better, clear my mind when I am stuck in certain thoughts, see myself better and of course puts me in the position to not overthinking whether I may or may not eat something, so every time I go out I can allow myself a cheat without feel bad.

As chef (or better big eater)

To be honest I'm more a good eater than a chef!
I literally like every kind of food, starting from the starters, passing through the vegetables, meat, fish, every king of vegetarian protein and ending with the dessert obviously. I love this part of myself, cause I like to make a parallel beetwen my stomach and my mind and if I have an open stomach I woul think that I have also an open mind.
Skipping this philosophical part, I love taste different foods caming from different paths, different kind of preparation, different raw mattters, different cultures. As in all the sections of my life I love try, explore my limits (maybe insects is the food that I haven't already tried) and discover news. I'have been lucky, I knew a lot of people caming from different country, with food cultures really different one to each other, thus I've had the opportunity to taste amazing exquisiteness.
Before you think that I'm just a gluttonous, I try also to express myself making new dishies, inventing new recipes, doing home made pasta and so on. Also now that I finally have a dishwasher, I can avoid the worst part of cooking, so I'll be more active.
I hope I'll be able to show some of mine recipes and several healthy/junk meals.

As traveller

Like a lot of people, I really love travel arround the world, both for just few days and for weeks (not so often unfortunatly). See beautiful places, landscape, meet new interesting coltures and amazing people expand and feel your soul, makes you a better person, gives you a wider awareness.
I'm always uncertain if wanna go abroad or visit all the cities in Italy, go in a big capital or see all the small reality, and it's tough choosing every time, how you make your decisions? I already have a list of all my next stops but everytime I talk with my friend a new amazng places cames out and I have to add it to my list, stretching it if it's still possible. Anyway I hope to make a section where I will be able to show my places, what I saw, who I encountered, I would like to share my experience as much as possible.
Of course money and time are the worst enemies for a traveller, but don't give up! There is always a way to do what you really want to do, you just need to find your one.
