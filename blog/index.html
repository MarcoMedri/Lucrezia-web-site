Short presentation

My name Is Lucrezia, I was born in Italy in 1991, exactly in Colle Val d'elsa, a small city in Tuscany.

As designer

I have always loved when useful things are also beautiful and simple and that the reason that pushed me to study design, in order to be able to create new beautiful things. I am a positive girl, straightforward, full of energy and always ready to learn new things. I am also a hard worker and the teamwork is my habitat.

As "fit/sport" girl

I love practice every type of sport, I never step back in front of new opportunities. I usually prefer team workouts instead of individual ones, I think that share the suffering allow you to go further your limits and is good meet new people as well.
I also like practise out of the gym, running and team sports as well like for istance volleyball or moreover beachvolley (To be honest I have played volleyball for several years ).
In the end I think that mobility is essential for an healthy life, so I started to be passionate about pilates, and after a while I decided to try to become an instructor. Therefore I started a very good course in Bologna and in the end I obtained my certificate, and now I have the licence to teach it.

As chef (or better good eater)

To be honest I'm more a good eater than a chef, I like every kind of food, starting from the starter, passing through the vegetables, meat, and so on, ending with the dessert obviously.
I hope to show you some of mine recepie and several healthy/junk meals.

As traveller

Like a lot of people, I really love travell arround the word, both for few days and for weeks (not so often unfortunatly) in order to see beautiful places and meet a lot of interesting and amazing coltures.
I hope you enjoy the map of the place that I visited in my life!
